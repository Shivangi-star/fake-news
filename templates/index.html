          Questions, answered.

          01 How does the forensic scanner decide real vs fake?

          Three passes — pixel forensics, claim reasoning, and a weighted verdict — produce a 0–100 credibility score and a label you can explain.

          02 Which file formats can I upload?

          Only JPEG, PNG or WEBP images are supported. Image must be under 10 MB.

          03 Is a high score a guarantee the content is authentic?

          No. This is AI-assisted analysis. Always verify with primary sources and trusted fact-checkers.

          04 Do you store the media I upload?

          No. Files are processed in memory for the scan and are not saved to disk on this server.

          05 Can the scanner be wrong?

          Yes. Novel edits, missing context, or outdated training can produce errors. Treat results as one input in your review.
